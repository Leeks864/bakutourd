Five Finger Mountain & Pink Lake Tour

The Five Finger Mountain & Pink Lake Tour showcases two of Azerbaijan’s most unusual natural wonders. Five Finger Mountain (“Besh Barmag”) is a striking rocky peak that looks like a giant hand rising from the landscape, long considered a sacred place and a symbol of strength. The Pink Lake (Masazir Lake) enchants with its vivid pink waters, colored by salt-loving microalgae, and is especially photogenic in spring and summer.

This tour is a favorite for photographers, nature lovers, and anyone seeking an easy day trip from Baku. Along the way, you’ll see dramatic landscapes, learn local legends, and experience the surreal atmosphere of Azerbaijan’s unique salt lake.

Highlights of the Five Finger Mountain & Pink Lake Tour include:

  • Five Finger Mountain (Besh Barmag): Climb or photograph this iconic peak and enjoy panoramic views of the surrounding plains.
  • Pink Lake (Masazir Lake): Marvel at its color, walk along the salt flats, and take spectacular photos.
  • Local Legends: Hear ancient stories and myths connected to the mountain and lake.
  • Salt Extraction Sites: See how salt is harvested from the lake in traditional ways.
  • Picnic Spot: Relax and enjoy lunch with a view of the lake or mountain.

Whether you’re after natural beauty, spiritual sites, or simply a fun day out, this tour is a perfect way to explore a different side of Azerbaijan.
